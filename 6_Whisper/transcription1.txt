 Whisper is an automatic speech recognition, or ASR, system developed by OpenAI. ASR systems translate spoken language into written text, bridging the gap between human speech and machine understanding. The Whisper model boasts an impressive training foundation built upon 680,000 hours of multilingual and multitask supervised data. But what can Whisper do? You're about to head into an onboarding session, and it's packed with ideas and information you need to remember. Typing or writing down everything you hear is a slow process, but Whisper comes to your rescue. Easily capturing every detail, Whisper transforms spoken content into accessible written text. Whisper can also help with employee support, language learning, medical transcription, and smart home automation. And that's just a glimpse into the extraordinary world of OpenAI's Whisper model.
